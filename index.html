Process of adoption

Step 1

Fill in our application form

In order to ensure that the animals in the shelter find suitable new homes and to confirm the applicant's ability to adopt the animals, we require all the applicants to complete an online application form.

Our staff will review your application and will inform you of the result by email and inbox of our website within 5 business days.

View Application Form >

Step 2

Wait for our response to your application

During the period of background reviewing and evaluating, you can make an appointment to visit our shelter and meet the pet you are applying for or other animals. This is NOT mandetory and will not affect the final decision of your application.

Please DONOT purchase any supplies or products for the pet you are applying for until you have received the results of our review, as we will NOT reimburse any associated expenses during or after your application even it is not approved. even if it is not approved.

Log in to check application status >

Step 3

Adaptation Week

After your application is approved, we will confirm your information and schedule a date to start temporary adoption with you, please note that you have not officially adopted your pet yet at this time.

From the first day of temporary adoption until 7 days later, the pet will be in your home (or any environment they will live in) for an adaptation week. The purpose of this is to see if the pet can adapt to its new environment and get along well with other people or animals living in the same environment. After the adaptation week, we will evaluate the pet's performance and you will be able to sign a formal adoption agreement after passing the evaluation.

Learn more about adaptation week >

Step 4

Welcome your new family member

After the successful completion of the adaptation week, we will schedule a date with you to sign an adoption agreement. You will be able to take your pet home the day the agreement is signed.
At the same time, we will provide you with a list of our recommended supplies customized for the pet based on their performance at the shelter.

Thank you for adopting, and we hope you will welcome your new family member!

Extra

Occasional return visits

Finding new homes for the animals in the shelter is not the destination for us, we want them to live happily with their adopters in their home.

To that end, we will communicate with you and make occasional visits to see how the pets are doing. This may be done through on-site visits, phone calls, online video chat (e.g. Zoom, etc.).

About Us

SLG Shelter

SLG Animal Shelter is a volunteer-initiated animal shelter and rescue program. All animals will be vaccinated and given the necessary care and rescue measures before they are available for adoption to the public.

SLG is a large, fully equipped shelter with 3 veterinarians, 10 pet caregivers and trainers, as well as volunteers to care for the animals. SLG Shelter has good communication with local pet protection organizations, and will carry out pet adoption activities and other related activities from time to time.

We care for animals and are responsible for them.

Our Mission

SLG Animal Shelter would like to provide the best for pets currently living in the shelter, but also those who have already found a new home.

We try to provide a warm and comfortable environment for the animals. With over 100 animals currently living at our shelter, keeping them all healthy and happy is a challenge, but thanks to donations from local organizations and the public, we are able to provide ideal services to the animals and potential adopters.

We hope to meet sincere and kind people love animals. If you would like to help, please consider making a donation, we appreciate your contribution.

Animal Rescue

The shelter maintains a positive and frequent communication with local animal associations and rescue team. Besides sheltering, we are also doing our best to save more animals and we are always calling for volunteers to join the team.

If you want to get involved, please send us an email or contact us in possible ways on our contact page.
